The Best Movies Acording to Alek

My top 3 movies of all time

Saving Private Ryan

Directed by Steven Spielverg, who realy captures true events of the WW2 and translate them into a immersive experience of loyalty, honor and humanity.

Sin City

Writen and Co-Directed by Frank Miller, featuring also Quentin Tarantino and Robert Rodriguez. It paints a broken city and the corruption between its habitants with a dark atmosphera and brilliant script, which translates beautifly a graphic novel into the large screen

V for Vendetta

Featuring Hugo Weaving and simply one of the best perfonces by Natalie Portman, V for Vendetta, a dystopian political action film that points to the curruption of mankind an the role goverment plays for every society with its habitants as pawns. In this movie, it's its narrative that envolves you through a definetly poetic argument while forces you to reject everything you know and open your eyes to all what's wrong with the world.
